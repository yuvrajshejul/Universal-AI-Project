Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)

Description: PMMVY is a maternity benefit program that provides cash incentives to pregnant and lactating women.

Key Features

  • Financial assistance for health and nutrition.
  • Conditional cash transfer for the first living child.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Pregnant and lactating women.
  • First living child.

Application Process: Register at the Anganwadi center or through the official website.

Contact Information: For more details, contact the Ministry of Women and Child Development.
