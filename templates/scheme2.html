National Health Mission (NHM)

Description: NHM aims to provide accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare to the rural population, especially women and children.

Key Features

  • Strengthening health infrastructure.
  • Maternal and child health services.
  • Prevention and control of communicable and non-communicable diseases.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Focus on rural and vulnerable populations.

Application Process: Implemented at the state level; individuals can access services through primary health centers.

Contact Information: For state-specific information, contact the respective State Health Department.
