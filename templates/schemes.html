SCHEMES

Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY)

Ayushman Bharat PMJAY is a health insurance scheme that aims to provide financial protection to over 100 million families for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization.

National Health Mission (NHM)

NHM aims to provide accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare to rural population, especially women and children.

Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)

PMMVY is a maternity benefit program that provides cash incentives to pregnant and lactating women.

Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana (RSBY)

RSBY is a health insurance scheme for below-poverty-line (BPL) families, providing coverage for hospitalization expenses.
