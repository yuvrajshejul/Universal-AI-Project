Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY)

Description: Ayushman Bharat PMJAY is a health insurance scheme that aims to provide financial protection to over 100 million families for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization.

Key Features

  • Cashless coverage for medical expenses.
  • Coverage for pre-existing diseases from day one.
  • Beneficiary can avail services in any empaneled hospital across India.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Socio-economic caste census (SECC) based identification.
  • Families listed in SECC database.

Application Process:

Interested individuals can apply online through the official website or visit the nearest Common Service Center (CSC).
