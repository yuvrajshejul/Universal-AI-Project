Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana (RSBY)

Description: RSBY is a health insurance scheme for below-poverty-line (BPL) families, providing coverage for hospitalization expenses.

Key Features

  • Smart card-based cashless transactions.
  • Coverage for pre-existing conditions.
  • Inpatient treatment in empaneled hospitals.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Below-poverty-line (BPL) families.
  • Unorganized sector workers.

Application Process: Register at the Anganwadi center or through the official website.

Contact Information: For more details, contact the Ministry of Women and Child Development.
